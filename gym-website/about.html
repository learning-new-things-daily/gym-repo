About IronPeak Gym

Our story, mission, team, and the values that make us the city's most trusted fitness center.

Our Story

Built by Fitness Enthusiasts, for Fitness Enthusiasts

IronPeak Gym was founded in 2015 by former competitive athlete Mike Torres with a simple but powerful vision: create a gym where every member feels welcomed, motivated, and empowered to achieve their best self.

What started as a modest 2,000 sq ft space with 12 members has grown into a 15,000 sq ft state-of-the-art fitness complex serving over 5,000 active members with 25+ certified trainers and more than 200 pieces of premium equipment.

Our Journey

2015

Grand Opening

IronPeak Gym opened its doors with 12 founding members and 5 trainers in a 2,000 sq ft space.

2017

First Expansion

Added Yoga and CrossFit studios, bringing total space to 7,000 sq ft with 15 trainers.

2019

Award-Winning

Voted "Best Gym in Muscle City" by Fitness Magazine readers for the first time.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched online coaching platform and mobile app with 500+ active online clients.

2023

Full Renovation

Expanded to 15,000 sq ft with new steam bath, sauna, and premium lounge area.

2024

5,000+ Members

Reached the milestone of 5,000 active members with 25 certified trainers on staff.
